About Me

I am Marco Di Luch, a professional circus artist with over ten years of experience performing in circuses, hotels, dinner shows, and events around the world.

I am aerialist with mains Aerial Pole and Aerial Straps acts. I also perform Aerial Rope and Ground Pole acts. I have experience working with acro duos and aerial duos, and extra skills like LED and Fire Staff Manipulation for receptions and events.

I have worked with companies like Cirque Du Soleil and Club Med Resort partnership, Cirque Mondial in France, Circus Espinol Rumania, ALAR dinner show Greece, Cirque Achille Zavatta Mauritius, Cabaret Edition Barcelona, Sendaviva Park Spain, Luain Producciones, Serv√≠an Circus Argentina and more.

Also I worked as an independent coach and in circus schools like Trapeze Arts inc USA, Flying Monkeys Ireland.

I am fluent in English, Spanish, Portuguese, and French, helping me work effectively with international work teams and colleagues.
